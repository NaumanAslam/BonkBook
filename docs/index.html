// faq

You have questions.
We have slaps.

Will this break my MacBook? +
BonkBook only reads the accelerometer — it doesn't do anything to your hardware. The slapping, however, is entirely your decision and your warranty claim.
Does it work on Intel Macs? +
BonkBook is built for Apple Silicon (M1 and later). Intel Macs have a different accelerometer setup and aren't supported right now.
Why does it need admin permission? +
macOS requires root access to read raw sensor data from the accelerometer. A tiny background daemon runs as root to handle this — the main app runs as a normal user. You grant permission once and it's done.
What's the difference between free and paid? +
The free version gives you 10 slaps across all sessions to try it out. Paid unlocks unlimited slaps and all future sound packs for a one-time $7.
How do I get my download after paying? +
After purchase on Gumroad, you'll get an email with a download link for the full version DMG. Download, drag to Applications, done.
Will it slow my Mac down? +
Nope. The sensor reads at ~400Hz but the daemon is extremely lightweight. Your MacBook won't even notice.
